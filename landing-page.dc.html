No. Module 0 starts from absolute zero — platform setup, goal-setting, and the core vocabulary you need before touching a single campaign. The curriculum is designed to take you from zero to job-ready.

How long do I have access to the course?

Lifetime. Enroll once and the content is yours. Modules are updated as Amazon changes its ad platform — you don't pay again for structural updates.

Go at your own speed. There are no deadlines. Professional and VIP members also get access to our community Discord where you can ask questions any time — the pace is yours to control.

Monthly plans cancel any time before renewal. No penalties, no awkward support calls. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.
