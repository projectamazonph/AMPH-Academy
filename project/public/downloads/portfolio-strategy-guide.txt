=== PORTFOLIO STRATEGY GUIDE ===

WHAT IS A CAMPAIGN PORTFOLIO?
A group of campaigns sharing a budget. When one campaign under-spends, others can use the remaining budget.

PORTFOLIO STRUCTURE (60/20/10/10 MODEL):

Spend Allocation:
- 60% — Core campaigns (best performers, stable ACoS)
- 20% — Growth campaigns (scaling, testing new keywords)
- 10% — Defensive campaigns (protect brand terms)
- 10% — Experimental campaigns (new products, markets)

BUDGET PACING:

| Phase | Allocation | Goal |
|-------|-----------|------|
| Days 1-3 | 35% of monthly | Data gathering, aggressive |
| Days 4-10 | 25% of monthly | Optimization, adjustments |
| Days 11-20 | 25% of monthly | Stability, scale winners |
| Days 21-30 | 15% of monthly | Evaluate, prepare next month |

SEASONAL ADJUSTMENTS:
- Q1: Reduce budget 20% (post-holiday dip)
- Q2: Maintain (spring growth)
- Q3: Increase 30% (preparation for Q4)
- Q4: Increase 50-100% (holiday peak)
