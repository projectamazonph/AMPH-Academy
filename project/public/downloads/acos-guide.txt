=== ACoS & PROFITABILITY GUIDE ===

WHAT IS ACoS?
Advertising Cost of Sale = Spend ÷ Sales × 100

Example: ₱5,000 spend → ₱25,000 sales = 20% ACoS

TARGET ACoS BY PRODUCT TYPE:

| Product Type | Target ACoS | Strategy |
|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Low Margin (15-20%) | 10-15% | Tight bidding, exact match focus |
| Mid Margin (20-35%) | 15-25% | Balanced, phrase + exact |
| High Margin (35%+) | 20-30% | Aggressive, broad + exact |
| Launch/New | 30-50% | Acceptance phase, gather data |

BREAK-EVEN ACoS CALCULATION:
Selling Price: ₱1,000
Product Cost: ₱300
Amazon Fees: ₱150
Shipping: ₱100
Total Cost: ₱550
Profit per unit: ₱450
Break-even ACoS = 450 / 1,000 = 45%

ACoS DIAGNOSTIC FLOW:
1. High ACoS + Low Spend → Check bids (too high)
2. High ACoS + High Spend → Check targeting (too broad)
3. Low ACoS + Low Spend → Check impressions (too restrictive)
4. Low ACoS + High Spend → Optimizing well, scale up
