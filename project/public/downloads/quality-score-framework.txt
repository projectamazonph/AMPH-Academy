=== QUALITY SCORE FRAMEWORK ===

WHAT IS QUALITY SCORE?
Amazon's estimation of your ad relevance, measured on a 1-10 scale. Higher scores = lower CPCs + better ad placement.

THREE FACTORS:

1. Click-Through Rate (CTR) — Weight: HIGH
- Expected CTR based on keyword and position
- Historical CTR for your ads in that placement
- Landing page relevance to the search query

2. Ad Relevance — Weight: MEDIUM
- How closely your keywords match your ad copy
- How closely your ad copy matches your landing page
- Keyword match type alignment

3. Landing Page Experience — Weight: MEDIUM
- Page load speed
- Product availability (in stock)
- Product detail page completeness
- Positive reviews and ratings

FEEDBACK LOOP:
Higher Quality Score → Lower CPC → More Impressions → Higher CTR → Higher Score

QUICK WINS:
- Match keywords to ad copy exactly
- Use exact match for your best-converting terms
- Ensure landing pages have complete product descriptions
- Improve page load speed (compress images)
